### Getting Support

1. **Search First** - Check existing issues and discussions before asking
2. **Be Specific** - Include version numbers, error messages, and steps to reproduce
3. **Provide Context** - Share your use case and what you're trying to accomplish
4. **Stay Engaged** - Respond to follow-up questions to help us help you

- **Helpful** - Provide constructive feedback and support

We're committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for all community members. Please be respectful in all interactions.
